(ii) Negotiate, at User’s request, a Fare that is lower than the pre-arranged Fare (each of (i) and (ii) herein “Negotiated Fare”).

SMARTCABS shall consider all such requests from the Driver to be in good faith. SMARTCABS agrees to remit to the Driver on at least a weekly basis:
